About Us

Our Mission

Cold Plunge Timer was created to help people practice cold exposure safely and mindfully. We believe the benefits of ice baths — improved circulation, reduced inflammation, mental resilience, and better recovery — should be accessible to everyone without expensive subscriptions or apps that collect your personal data.

Why We Built This

Most cold plunge apps require sign-up, push notifications, and cloud sync — none of which are necessary for a timer. We wanted something simple: set your water temperature, see a safe duration range, start your timer, and breathe. No account, no ads tracking your health data, no complexity.

The built-in box breathing guide is there because controlled breathing is essential for managing the cold shock response. We believe a tool should help you stay calm, not add more noise.

Our Values

  • Privacy first. Everything runs in your browser. Your session history never leaves your device.
  • Safety focused. Temperature recommendations are conservative and clearly labeled.
  • Simplicity. A timer should be a timer. No feature bloat, no distractions.
  • Free, forever. Cold exposure basics should not require a subscription.

Who We Are

We are a small team of developers and cold exposure enthusiasts who believe in building tools that respect users. We build pure front-end applications that solve real problems without harvesting data or locking features behind paywalls.
